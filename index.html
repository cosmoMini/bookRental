VISION

We promise to deliver the slightest aid to the world, by providing books to the needy or to those who can't afford the books at higher/original cost.

MISSION

Many desire to learn and grow, but due to poverty or lack of resources they can't. We desire to provide them books with much lower cost than the market, so that they can purchase and learn. We do not say that this will help everyone to get educated, but atleast it will make a little change.

These many children have no access to books, just because they can't afford original/new books from the shop.
